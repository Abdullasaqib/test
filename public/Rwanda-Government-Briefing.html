Executive Summary

Rwanda has positioned itself as Africa's Singapore—the cleanest cities, the most business-friendly environment, the boldest vision. But the next phase of growth requires something different: a generation of young founders who can build the companies of tomorrow.

NEXT_ is the world's first AI-native entrepreneurship program for students aged 9-16. In 12 weeks, students discover real problems, validate with real customers, build real products using AI tools, and launch real businesses. This isn't a classroom exercise—it's a founder factory.
